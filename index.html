The flagship volume from the Observatory — a five-decade assessment of the World Bank in Nepal, with candid critique and a forward-looking agenda.

The World Bank in Nepal: Towards New Horizons

Dr. Shakti P. Shrestha

A comprehensive, multidimensional examination of the World Bank's engagement in Nepal over five decades. Blending empirical rigor with candid critique, this volume traces the evolution from infrastructure-focused aid to holistic, inclusive development — and charts a bold path forward for Nepal's future.
