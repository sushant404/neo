Meet the Author

Dr. Shakti P. Shrestha

Economist, author, and policy practitioner — advancing rigorous, independent research on Nepal's development.

SPS
Dr. Shakti P. Shrestha
Ph.D. Economist · Development Policy Specialist

Seasoned economist with over three decades of expertise in public finance, macroeconomic modeling, and development policy. A former professional staff member at the World Bank's Nepal Office, he also spent two decades in Nepal's central bank research department. Author of six books on Nepal's development challenges, and an economist at Tribhuvan University.
